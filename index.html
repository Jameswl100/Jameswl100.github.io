Walchester Way

This 6.6 mile route is suitable for group 1 and has 3 potentially hazardous road crossings. Care must be taken when crossing the A435 just after 3 km. Cars are often travelling at speed as they exit the dual-carriageway section of the A435. The second hazard is the crossing of the Henley Road (A4189) at the 4th km. Finally, there is a less hazardous crossing of the A435 just before the 6th km.

Beoley Hill 4 miles

This 4 mile loop is suitable for groups 1, 2 and 3. The only hazard is the Icknield Road crossing at the 4th km. The final approach through Church Hill to return to AV park can be confusing. On no account should you follow Beth, even if she is super-confidant of the route.
